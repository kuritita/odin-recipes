Gazpacho

Description

This is a famous Andalusian cold soup perfect for a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Green peppers
  • Cucumber
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bread slice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash vegetables
  2. Throw vegetables into a blender
  3. Blend
  4. Add bread and salt
  5. Blend again
  6. Add olive oil
  7. You guessed it: blend again
  8. Transfer to a bowl
  9. Transfer to the fridge
  10. Wait for about an hour
  11. Ready!
