Salmorejo

Description

This is another famous Andalusian cold soup but different from gazpacho.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Bread slice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Boiled egg
  • Jamón ibérico

Steps

  1. Boil an egg
  2. Wash tomatoes
  3. Throw tomatoes and garlic into a blender
  4. Blend
  5. Add bread and salt
  6. Blend again
  7. Add olive oil
  8. You guessed it: blend again
  9. Transfer to a bowl
  10. Transfer to the fridge
  11. Wait for about an hour
  12. Peel boiled egg
  13. Grate boiled egg
  14. Cut jamón ibérico into small bits
  15. Throw boiled egg and jamón ibérico over the salmorejo
  16. Ready!
