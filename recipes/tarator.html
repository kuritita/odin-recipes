Tarator

Description

This is a famous Bulgarian cold soup but I only have a vague idea of how it's made

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Cucumber
  • Some spices, dill or something like that
  • Some kind of vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • I think some walnuts?
  • Water??

Steps

  1. Mix some yogurt and water
  2. Slice cucumber into tiny cubes
  3. Add cucumbers to yogurt-water mix
  4. At some point throw in some spices
  5. Add some walnuts too
  6. Add salt
  7. And add some oil, I guess
  8. Transfer to the fridge
  9. Wait for about an hour
  10. And that's about it, I think
